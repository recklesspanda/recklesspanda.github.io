The Draw of Snapchat

Snapchat has taken the social media world by storm, and there doesn't seem to be any end in sight...

Snapchat is a mobile application that was first released in late 2011. The basic premise is quite simple, you can send pictures to other Snapchat users that disappear after a set amount of time, often no more than ten seconds.
