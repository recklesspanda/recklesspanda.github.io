"The Scotti's"

January 13, 2018

A few months ago I found myself looking through PRØJECT: Denim - business idea by Austin Scotti to provide unique and customizable jeans to customers. Through his Instagram, I found out he customizes all his pieces to be uniquely his.

This struck a chord with me. So, I got my hands on an old sewing machine, taught myself how to sew/reconstruct jeans, and ended up with this: a foundation to develop my own style from. Starting with cues straight from PRØJECT: Denim I made these, a set of vintage Japanese made selvedge denim aptly named "The Scotti's" to pay credit to their original designer.

Features:

  • Tapered from the knee down
  • Calf seams
  • Right Dickie style cargo pocket
  • Off-set rear pocket to showcase original wash color
  • Extended ankle hems
  • Split inner-ankle seams
