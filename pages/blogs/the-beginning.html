The Beginning...

September 10, 2017

Welcome to my first ever blog post!  This is the formal relaunch of my website.  I've been grinding these past few weeks to get content ready to roll out, and I'm excited to begin sharing with the world!

The point of this newsletter is for me to get my thoughts out into the public space, and hopefully connect with you if you're interested in what I'm up to.  My goal is to release a new blog post each week.  The topics will vary, but will be centered around my experiences in technology, entrepreneurship, and leadership.  A lot of it may be me talking out loud to myself, but hey, maybe someone out there is interested in what I have to say.

Within the next week I'll have a page on my website so you can sign up for my newsletter via email.  Make sure to follow me on Instagram and Twitter, and be looking for next week's blog post. Thanks!

--
