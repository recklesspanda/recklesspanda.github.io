About

Hi, my name is Stephen Ritchie and I'm a computer science student at the University of Kentucky. One of my favorite things to do is examine the world around me, and see how I can apply my technology skills to improve something. Through different mentors I've interacted with, I've narrowed down my passions to the following...

  • technology
  • entrepreneurship

This is a site that I've built to share my ideas with the global audience. This site will most likely evolve and change over time, so make sure to check back every once in a while to see what's new. And if you find yourself sharing these same values that I do, get in contact with me. I love bouncing ideas around and building a network of like minded individuals.
