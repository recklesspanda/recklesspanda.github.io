About

The Bluegrass Angels are a group of accredited angel investors based in Lexington, investing solely in Kentucky based startups since 2008. We are a volunteer group of skilled and experienced business leaders who find, fund and mentor innovative, high-tech Kentucky startups seeking financial viability and long-term success. This includes working with the University of Kentucky to help commercialize ongoing research.

INVESTORS

All members must be accredited investors as defined in Regulation D under the Securitites Act of 1993, or SEC Rule 501, and must be able to sustain the financial risk inherent in their investments.

Interested parties must be able to meet these requirements and may apply for a membership at any time. Prior to making a final committment, prospective members are encouraged to attend one of our monthly meetings and experience the fellowship of the group.
